RIMAC HYPERCARS

Rimac Automobili was founded in Sveta Nedelja, near Zagreb in 2009.

In 2011 Rimac introduced the world to the Concept One at the Frankfurt Motor Show. Soon after breaking several world records with their innovative technologies.

In 2014 The Rimac Concept_One becomes FIA Formula E's official Race Director Car and Rimac Automobili awarded the best Croatian employer.

During 2015 Rimac has over 100 employees and to 250 in 2017.
